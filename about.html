About me

Graphic Designer and Illustrator

Evgeniy Synenchenko

Kyiv, Ukraine

I create engaging and attractive graphic designs and illustrations for websites, print, blogs, and social networks, using Adobe Creative Suite, Figma, Canva and other tools.

With more than 15 years of experience in the field of graphic design and illustration, I have developed a versatile and innovative style that adapts to different projects and clients. I have worked on packaging and licensed products, presentations, posters, billboards, flyers, applications, and website design for various industries and sectors. I am passionate about creating visual stories that communicate effectively and inspire audiences.
